https://github.com/cucumber/cucumber-jvm/pull/3016

Cucumber JSON Report is in maintaince mode.
The json output  will be supported, but not changed any more.
The replacement, Cucumber Messages, is under development.
https://github.com/cucumber/messages/

cucumber-js:
Sharding https://github.com/cucumber/cucumber-js/pull/2600 (contributed by the community)
Config files as TypeScript https://github.com/cucumber/cucumber-js/pull/2709
The HTML formatter got redesigned for the better (applies to other platforms too)
User-authored plugins support (TBA but aiming to get this out over the weekend)

https://github.com/cucumber/cucumber-node is taking shape - I'll do a long post on this in the Spring but for now early adopters should have a good enough time.

Other than that, lots of architectural tetris to unblock things. I'm rewriting the parallel runtime so I can ship about 3 dfifferent things that don't play nicely with it today.

With formatters and messages, Rien hinted at it already, I'd elaborate: we've been dogfooding it for the first-party formatters and building up the documentation, tooling etc through that, and the fruits of that will be available to users soon.

Oh, and we relaunched Cucumber Reports https://reports.cucumber.io/
